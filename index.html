Welcome to Mr. Langley's Science Wiki

___The purpose of this website is to provide students and parents with access to lessons and handouts they may have missed and to connect them with resources to ensure their success in the study of Science. Please use the links provided to access course information and materials relevant to your studies this semester.

Science Links

_ _This page contains links that may be helpful to you if you're interested in honing your Science skills, pursuing Scientific job/volunteer opportunities or if you're just plain into Science.

Official Sites

Ontario Secondary School Curriculum Documents
Grade 9 - 10
Grade 11 - 12
_ _The official government-published documents that dictate what students learn in specific courses/grades.
Ottawa-Carleton District School Board
http://www.ocdsb.ca
_ _The official website of the OCDSB.

Journal of Nature
http://www.nature.com/nature/index.html
_ _One of the most reputable and trusted sources of cutting edge Science news in the world (often difficult to understand without a university education).
Scientific American
http://www.scientificamerican.com/
_ _Popular Science magazine that has done an excellent job at making difficult-to-understand research accessible to the general public.

Student Opportunities

CurioCity
http://www.explorecuriocity.org/
_ _Articles, answers to popular Science questions as well as career guidance and involvement opportunities for students (and scientists) of all ages.
Scholarships Canada
http://www.scholarshipscanada.com/
_ _A great site through which to earn money for academic achievements and artistic accomplishment. Not all scholarships are for graduating students planning to attend university! Explore the site for opportunities tailored to you.

For Fun

ASAP Science (YouTube Channel)
https://www.youtube.com/user/AsapSCIENCE
_ _Short, art-based answers to burning Science questions like what are goosebumps? and why do humans age?
ThinkGeek
http://www.thinkgeek.com
_ _If you're looking for nerdy or science-based clothing, accessories, trinkets or gifts look no further.
